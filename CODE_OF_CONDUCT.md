# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want `acp-ws-bridge` to be a respectful, constructive, and welcoming project for contributors, users, and maintainers.

Participants in this project are expected to:

- be respectful and professional
- assume good intent while discussing technical disagreements
- give actionable, specific feedback
- be welcoming to contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels

## Unacceptable behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, reviews, and other public interactions:

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal attacks, insults, or abusive language
- deliberately hostile or disruptive behavior
- publishing someone else's private information without permission
- bad-faith trolling or repeated derailment of technical discussion

## Maintainer responsibilities

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

When possible, maintainers should explain moderation decisions clearly and respectfully.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces and to public interactions that are clearly connected to this repository and its community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainer privately through GitHub:

- https://github.com/ytthuan

Please include as much context as you can so the report can be reviewed fairly.

## Enforcement

Reports will be reviewed in good faith. Maintainers may take any action they believe is necessary to protect the project and its participants, including warning, editing, removing content, or banning repeat offenders.
